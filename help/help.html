How to Play


In order to play the Commarite quiz, begin by clicking on "BEGIN" where you will be taken to the questions page. Here you will find a series of questions relating to lore surrounding the denizens of the dark city Commorragh. To find out more about the Drukhari that inhabit Commorragh click here.


Once you have read the question, select one of the multiple choice answers by clicking on the corresponding button. Once all questions have been answered, you will be taken to a results page where you will be judged by his eminence.


Terminology


  • "Return to Port" : This refers to returning to the home page
